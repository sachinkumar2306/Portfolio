KIIT University

KIIT University, KIIT University is an autonomous Private Institution dedicated to maintain excellent academic performance. Since its establishment in 2011, KIIT University has been actively working as a regular franchisee of KIIT University Education Limited, Singapore. KIIT University KIIT has an established track record of Academic excellence, within the Networks of KIIT University – Globally. We are supported by a team of dedicated Professors, Lecturers, Counselors, meticulous management with a caring H.R. support service. KIIT University is today producing competitive Graduates, creating an enviable position among employers and Universities both Nationally and Internationally. KIIT University, today can proudly boast of having produced sustainable and marketable graduates in various IT and Business & Management programs.

About Programme

This post is written by Sachin Kumar

KIIT was established in 1992 as a vocational training centre with only two staff and 12 students. However, it took shape as an institution only in 1997, when degree engineering programme commenced. KIIT owes its existence to the vision of its founder Prof. Achyuta Samanta who, with no land, limited finances (only Rs. 5000/-) and little knowledge of higher education to create an institution for the benefit of the society. From these small beginnings, KIIT has developed into a leading university with a strong interdisciplinary culture and a commitment to teaching excellence.
